Michael Frohberg

I am 30 years old. I have an amazing wife, and a fanastic little girl. I have always found it incredibly difficult to write anything about myself; I lack any sort of objective view. To briefly summarize my history, I grew up on the sets of movies and television shows in New York City. I started out as a child actor, then branched out into working on crews from the age of 13. My day gig is as a Union Audio Mixer. I work mostly in reality tv as of late, but come from a strong narrative background. I look forward to incorporating what I learn, here at Full Sail, into developing dynamic mobile Apps geared toward Film & TV Production and those who aspire to enter that field.
